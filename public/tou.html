Conduct Nabo is about inclusion, belonging and looking after the people closest to you. We take care to protect the rights of all Nabo users and it’s fair to expect all members to follow the Code of Conduct and treat each other with respect. Code of Conduct Be honest Use your real name, photo and address. Join as an individual not as a family or business. Be truthful particularly when recommending services, selling items or providing information. Be kind Treat everyone with respect. If you disagree, do it in a respectful way. Everyone deserves a chance to be part of the conversation so don’t over post. Give people the benefit of the doubt. Be polite If you wouldn’t say it to your mum then don’t post it on Nabo. This includes using profanity, personally attacking someone or anything that could be seen as discriminatory. Take the time to add a photo and to fill out your profile. Recommend local businesses but don’t self promote, campaign or spam. Be helpful Answer questions and share relevant information. Help members in need. Give away or sell things you don’t need. Share information on safety issues, crime or local danger spots. Talk about things that matter to your local area. Spread the word about local events. Discuss community-wide issues without campaigning. We may monitor your use of Nabo to ensure that conduct is appropriate and in accordance with the Nabo Code of Conduct. Any behaviour that could be deemed offensive or inappropriate will not be tolerated.
