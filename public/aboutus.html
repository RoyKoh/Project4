About SG Kampung

What is SG Kampung?

SG Kampung is the free social network being used in thousands of Constituents across Singapore, helping residents to get the best out of their local area.

There are many reasons to join SG Kampung:

Gain and share local knowledge - Recommend and discover the best things around you, from local events and attractions, a trusted service or tradie, to the best coffee in town.

Create a safer Constituent - Create a support network of local people who can keep an eye on your home or pet while you're away. Grow a stronger neighbourhood together

Buy, Sell, Borrow - Find a kids second hand bike or toy, sell an unwanted item from around your home, or borrow something handy from your neighbours.

Who else is behind SG Kampung?

SG Kampung is a GA Student Project by Roy Koh for a online resident focal point in the new BTO flat he is shifting to.

SG Kampung is based in Singapore, Woodlands. It is looking towards building a team of people passionate about marrying technology with community to help build relationships between neighbours across Singapore.

Privacy

The privacy of our members is important to us. We want you to feel safe sharing information online that you would be happy to share over the fence with a neighbour.

  • We ask that all members use their real name when creating their account.
  • We ask that all members verify their address
  • We do not share your personal information with any advertisers or third parties
  • SG Kampung is protected by a password and encrypted by HTTPS
  • All data is stored in Singapore and is protected under Singaporen privacy laws
