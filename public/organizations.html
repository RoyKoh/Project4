Need more information?

Why did SG Kampung start?

SG Kampung was born out of the realisation that the sense of community we once experienced is struggling to exist in this technological age. SG Kampung makes that same technology bring us together again.

How is SG Kampung different from other social networks?

Members verify their residential address to ensure that you're only talking to people from your neighbourhood. SG Kampung is unique in that it is specifically designed to connect residents with each other and their Constituent.

How is SG Kampung funded?

SG Kampung is not funded now and is seeking funding.

Why should your organisation join SG Kampung?

Residents in your neighbourhood are already using SG Kampung to connect and discuss a range of local community issues including crime, safety, traffic and transport. Members are looking for organisations to join the conversation.

How much does it cost for an organisation to join SG Kampung?

SG Kampung is free for members and organisations.

How long does it take to set up an account on SG Kampung?

Organisations can have their SG Kampung account established within 3-5 business days. Posting a message to SG Kampung is similar to other social networks and can take as little as 30 seconds.

How is SG Kampung moderated?

Any member of SG Kampung can report a message if they believe the message is inappropriate, abusive or commercial. SG Kampung organisations can moderate reported content on their page. Reported content will also be sent to SG Kampung Admin and our Constituent Leads.

Can businesses join SG Kampung?

At this time SG Kampung’s focus is on connecting the residents of a Constituent with one another to create stronger and safer communities. We do not currently allow the promotion of businesses and services. We do believe that there are some great synergies between SG Kampung, residents and local businesses and will be looking into these relationships in the future.
